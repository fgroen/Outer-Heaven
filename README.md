# Outer-Heaven

A collection of MSX productions from **Outer Heaven**, including music disks, hardware projects, and other developments.

---

## Projects

### [Outer Sounds](outer-sounds/) - Music Disk (May 2024)

A collaborative music disk for MSX 2, featuring original compositions and visual effects created by Outer Heaven members. Released in **May 2024** as part of MSX Day - MaySiXth.

**[View Outer Sounds →](outer-sounds/)**

---

### [SIXPAC](sixpac/) - FM-PAC Clone (2024-2025)

A modern FM-PAC clone for the MSX, featuring a high-quality line-out, adjustable audio levels, and genuine YM2413 sound chip. Hardware available for preorder, with firmware updates released throughout **2024-2025**.

**Initial release:** January 2025  
**Latest firmware release:** December 2025 (with SuperSoniqs integration)

**[View SIXPAC →](sixpac/)**

---

## About Outer Heaven

Outer Heaven is a group of MSX enthusiasts dedicated to creating high-quality hardware and software for the MSX platform. Our projects range from music disks and demos to hardware cartridges and development tools.
